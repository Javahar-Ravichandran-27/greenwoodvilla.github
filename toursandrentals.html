Tours & Rentals

Tuk Tuk Rentals

Greenwood Villa provides tuk-tuk rental services for guests to explore Negombo at their own pace and convenience. This unique and local mode of transportation allows guests to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and experience the city's sights and sounds. Guests can rent a tuk-tuk directly from the front desk, with staff providing information on rates, terms, and necessary documentation. A valid driver's license is required. Greenwood Villa staff can recommend popular tourist spots, scenic routes, and attractions.

Rates may vary depending on factors like duration, fuel usage, and equipment. Guests should also understand local traffic rules and regulations before operating a tuk-tuk.

Scooter Rentals

Greenwood Villa offers scooter rental services for guests to explore Negombo and its surrounding areas. This convenient and flexible mode of transportation allows guests to navigate the city at their own pace, discover hidden gems, and easily access nearby attractions. Guests can inquire at the front desk or contact the villa directly for availability, rates, and requirements. On-site pickup and drop-off are provided, and staff can recommend popular routes and attractions. Prior experience and a valid driver's license are required.

Rates may vary based on duration, type, and equipment. Discussing rental terms, pricing, and insurance coverage with Greenwood Villa staff ensures a clear understanding of the rental agreement.

Car Rentals

Greenwood Villa offers car rental services for guests to explore Negombo and its surrounding areas with convenience and flexibility. Guests can choose from a variety of vehicles, including compact cars and SUVs, and request additional services like GPS navigation or child safety seats. Greenwood Villa staff provide guidance on popular routes, attractions, and scenic spots. Prior driving experience and a valid driver's license are required, and local traffic rules must be followed.

Rates may vary depending on duration, car type, and additional services. It is recommended to discuss rental terms, pricing, insurance coverage, and conditions with Greenwood Villa staff for a clear understanding of the rental agreement.
