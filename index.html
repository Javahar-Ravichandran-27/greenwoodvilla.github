About Negombo

Negombo, a vibrant coastal city on Sri Lanka's western coast, is known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and diverse culture.

It offers water sports enthusiasts and sunbathing opportunities, while the warm Indian Ocean waters provide opportunities for swimming, surfing, diving, and fishing.

Accomodations

Our Accomodations

Green Wood Villa, a charming accommodation option in Negombo, Sri Lanka, offers a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere for travelers seeking a peaceful getaway. The villa features well-appointed rooms with modern amenities and traditional Sri Lankan touches, offering spacious and cozy accommodations.

Guests can choose from standard, deluxe, and suites, each equipped with air conditioning, comfortable beds, en-suite bathrooms, and complimentary toiletries. The villa also features a lush garden and a refreshing swimming pool, providing a serene setting for leisurely walks or reflection.

The on-site restaurant serves delicious Sri Lankan and international dishes prepared with fresh local ingredients, allowing guests to indulge in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. The friendly staff at Green Wood Villa are attentive to guests' needs, assisting with transportation, excursions, and recommendations for local attractions. The villa's location is convenient for exploring nearby attractions, such as beaches, markets, historical sites, and religious landmarks. Overall, Green Wood Villa provides a peaceful and comfortable retreat in Negombo, combining warm hospitality, comfortable accommodations, and a tranquil ambiance.

Facilities

Our Facilities

Swimming Pool

Green Wood Villa's swimming pool, located in a lush garden, offers a serene oasis for guests to unwind and enjoy leisure time. The pool area is well-maintained and designed for relaxation, with sun loungers and umbrellas for sunbathing. The serene atmosphere and well-manicured landscaping add to the charm of the setting. The attentive staff ensures a comfortable and enjoyable experience.

Airport Transportation

Green Wood Villa provides airport transportation services for a hassle-free arrival and departure experience. Guests can enjoy airport pickup and drop-off services, with well-maintained, comfortable vehicles and knowledgeable staff. The staff prioritize safety, punctuality, and guest satisfaction. It's advisable to inform the villa in advance about flight details for smooth coordination.

24 Hours Desk

Green Wood Villa offers a 24-hour front desk service, providing round-the-clock assistance to guests. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, handling check-in, transportation, and other services. They also handle emergencies, maintain security, and provide guidance for guests. The villa's commitment to guest satisfaction is evident in their availability and commitment to providing excellent customer service.

Spa and Wellness

Green Wood Villa offers a rejuvenating spa and wellness experience, promoting relaxation, wellness, and inner balance. With skilled therapists and a serene environment, guests can choose from massages, body scrubs, facials, and aromatherapy sessions. The therapists use high-quality natural products for comfort and effectiveness. Guests can book sessions in advance and consult the front desk for personalized options.

Train Ticket Arrangements

Green Wood Villa provides assistance with train ticket arrangements for guests to explore Sri Lanka's scenic landscapes. The front desk staff is knowledgeable about train routes, schedules, and ticket availability, offering guidance and recommendations based on guests' preferences. They ensure smooth travel experiences by assisting guests with destination information, boarding procedures, and train station information.

Tourist Information

Green Wood Villa offers comprehensive tourist information for a memorable and enriching stay in Negombo, Sri Lanka. The staff provides detailed information on attractions, activities, excursions, dining, shopping, transportation, events, and security. They also offer recommendations for local customs and safety precautions. The villa's staff is knowledgeable and responsive, ensuring guests have a memorable and rewarding experience.
