Our Accomodations

Green Wood Villa, a charming accommodation option in Negombo, Sri Lanka, offers a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere for travelers seeking a peaceful getaway. The villa features well-appointed rooms with modern amenities and traditional Sri Lankan touches, offering spacious and cozy accommodations.

Guests can choose from standard, deluxe, and suites, each equipped with air conditioning, comfortable beds, en-suite bathrooms, and complimentary toiletries. The villa also features a lush garden and a refreshing swimming pool, providing a serene setting for leisurely walks or reflection.

The on-site restaurant serves delicious Sri Lankan and international dishes prepared with fresh local ingredients, allowing guests to indulge in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. The friendly staff at Green Wood Villa are attentive to guests' needs, assisting with transportation, excursions, and recommendations for local attractions. The villa's location is convenient for exploring nearby attractions, such as beaches, markets, historical sites, and religious landmarks. Overall, Green Wood Villa provides a peaceful and comfortable retreat in Negombo, combining warm hospitality, comfortable accommodations, and a tranquil ambiance.
